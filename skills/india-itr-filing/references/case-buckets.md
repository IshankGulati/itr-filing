## 1. Resident salary-only individual

- likely form:
  - `ITR-1`
  - sometimes `ITR-2` if a simplicity condition breaks
- common heads:
  - salary or pension
  - bank interest
- common source documents:
  - `Form 16`
  - `AIS`
  - `TIS`
  - `26AS`
  - bank interest certificate
- popular acquisition channels:
  - employer HR or payroll portal
  - bank netbanking
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - `Form 12BB`
  - `Form 10E` if arrears relief is involved

Keep this bucket only if the current AY `ITR-1` conditions actually fit. Check the current official page for total-income ceiling, agricultural-income limit, and any limited `112A` allowance.

## 2. Resident salary plus investments

- likely form:
  - often `ITR-2`
- common heads:
  - salary
  - capital gains
  - dividends
  - interest
  - agricultural income if present
  - virtual digital assets if present
- common source documents:
  - `Form 16`
  - broker capital-gains report
  - mutual-fund gain statement
  - dividend statement
  - `AIS`
  - `26AS`
- popular acquisition channels:
  - broker tax report
  - MFCentral
  - CAMS
  - KFintech
  - depository CAS
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - `CG`
  - `OS`
  - `VDA`
  - `EI`
  - `BFLA`
  - `CYLA`
  - `CFL`

## 3. Resident professional or proprietorship

- likely form:
  - usually `ITR-3`
- common heads:
  - business or profession
  - salary, capital gains, or other sources if also present
- common source documents:
  - invoices
  - receipt ledger
  - bank trail
  - `Form 16A`
  - books or accounting export
  - `AIS`
  - `26AS`
- popular acquisition channels:
  - accounting software
  - bank statements
  - client TDS certificates
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - `BP`
  - profit and loss or balance-sheet support where relevant
  - `Form 10-IEA` when regime choice for business matters
  - audit forms when applicable

## 4. Presumptive small business or profession

- likely form:
  - often `ITR-4`
- common heads:
  - presumptive business or profession
  - interest
  - limited ancillary income
- common source documents:
  - turnover or receipt support
  - bank trail
  - `Form 16A`
  - `AIS`
  - `26AS`
- popular acquisition channels:
  - invoice tool
  - accounting export
  - bank statements
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - presumptive computation support
  - `Form 10-IEA` when regime history matters

Check the current-law presumptive thresholds and digital-receipt conditions before locking this bucket in.

## 5. NRI or RNOR individual

- likely form:
  - often `ITR-2`
  - `ITR-3` if business or profession income exists
- common heads:
  - domestic salary or other sources
  - capital gains
  - foreign income or assets
  - FTC
- common source documents:
  - `AIS`
  - `26AS`
  - foreign broker statements
  - withholding reports
  - bank statements
  - prior-year return support
- popular acquisition channels:
  - broker annual statements
  - employer payroll
  - bank portals
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - `FA`
  - `FSI`
  - `TR`
  - `Form 67`

This skill supports Indian taxpayers with foreign investments or income. It does not attempt to prepare foreign-country returns.

## 6. HUF

- likely form:
  - `ITR-2`
  - `ITR-3`
  - `ITR-4`
- common heads:
  - property
  - capital gains
  - business or profession where relevant
- common source documents:
  - deed-level support
  - bank statements
  - investment reports
  - `AIS`
  - `26AS`
- popular acquisition channels:
  - bank portals
  - broker or registrar portals
  - mutual-fund service platforms

## 7. Firm or LLP

- likely form:
  - `ITR-5`
- common heads:
  - business or profession
  - capital gains or other sources when present
- common source documents:
  - books
  - bank statements
  - invoices
  - `Form 16A`
  - audit reports
- popular acquisition channels:
  - accounting software
  - bank portals
  - auditor workpapers
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - `Form 3CA-3CD` or `3CB-3CD`
  - `Form 3CEB` where applicable

## 8. Company

- likely form:
  - `ITR-6`
- common heads:
  - business income
  - investment income
  - international-tax items when relevant
- common source documents:
  - audited financials
  - tax-audit papers
  - TDS support
  - bank statements
- popular acquisition channels:
  - finance stack
  - ERP
  - auditor pack

## 9. Trust, AOP, BOI, or AJP

- likely form:
  - `ITR-5`
  - `ITR-7`
- common heads:
  - depends on entity and exemption position
- common source documents:
  - trust deed or constituting documents
  - books
  - exemption support
  - TDS support
- popular acquisition channels:
  - entity books
  - auditor or trustee records
- common extra forms or schedules:
  - exemption-linked schedules and statutory attachments as applicable
